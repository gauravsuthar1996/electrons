## The Challenge

<p style="text-align: justify;">
  Rajasthan being a tourist hub attracts people from arround the world to have experience the wonderful combination of heritage & natural beauty.<br />It has many tourists places which are daily visited by lots of people.<br />When people plan trip they can find & book hotels, restaurants online but not the places they want to visit. If there are 5-10 tourists place in a city a tourist have to go to each place and book tickets by standing in queue.<br />Tourist places have no facilities to book tickets online and plan the whole trip from home.<br />Tourist generally don't carry much cash with them as they fear of theft and robbery. There is also problem of small currency notes for purchasing tickets.
</p>[/et_pb_text][/et_pb_column][et_pb_column type="1_2" \_builder\_version="3.0.47" parallax="off" parallax_method="on"][et_pb_text \_builder\_version="3.0.64" background_size="initial" background_position="top_left" background_repeat="repeat"]

## The Solution

<p style="text-align: justify;">
  The online integrated e-ticketing portal will consist of both web and smartphone app.<br />People can book there tickets from home through android/iOS app or the website.<br />In the proposed plan the visitor can purchase tickets of individual tourist places or can plan a trip which will include the places he/she wants to see.<br />There will be list of all the tourism places of Rajasthan according to the city in which they are located.<br />There are many advantages of online integrated e-ticketing platform :<br />1. Hassle free online booking:<br />2. Single payment for all the tourist places:<br />3. Option to choose time of availability:<br />4. People will be able to rate the places and share to others through social media<br />5. Integration with Google map
